First Rain

Crafted using the age-old Deg & Bhapka distillation process, where the heart of the fragrance is mitti itself ‚Äì the very clay that holds the scent of the first monsoon showers.

Each batch takes almost a month to craft. And it can only be done during the dry months of the year, when the air carries no moisture, allowing the clay to bloom with true petrichor.

This seasonality means First Rain is often limited and sells out ‚Äî a natural rhythm we honour rather than fight.

The molecule that creates petrichor is naturally very volatile, which means it lasts only 4‚Äì5 hours. We could add modern aroma molecules to extend longevity, but with them, the soul of First Rain disappears.

So we made a conscious choice: purity over projection, authenticity over enhancement, joy over longevity.
